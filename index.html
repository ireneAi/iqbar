How To
Complete Course

Preparation before the session

Breadies will explain their needs for an upcoming session in their self-assement form, which will be sent to you ahead of your session. Have a read and be prepared!

Please be online 10 minutes ahead of your session's starting time.

All you need is a working Internet connection, earphones, working microphone, webcam and Skype. A good Internet connection is paramount for a good tutoring experience.
